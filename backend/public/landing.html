What is Converge?


Converge is an attempt to find somewhat of a reprieve from the problems faced by students to gain experience in the development world. The functionality of the app is divided into two parts : any person/organisation currently working on a project can list it on the application along with the required stacks and skills requirement for an applicant.

How it works?

Recruiters add their projects with relevant details



Applicants search for projects suiting their skillsets, and submit applications


Applications are reviewed by project owners, and selected applicants are contacted



For applicants


Applicants can search for projects based on their current location and skillset, and apply to multiple projects at the same time, and check the current status of their applications.

Converge is helping the individuals find relevant opportunities that suit their skills and interests.

  • Job seekers
  • Freelancers
  • Students
  • Hobbyists



For recruiters


Project owners can create project posts with relevant descriptions including project title, tentative timeline, skills required by applicants, stipend(if any), location etc.

Helping people who are recruiting teammates for group projects, hackathons, or hobby projects, and professional recruiters who are looking to hire potential employees for roles in their organization.

  • Team leads in group competitions, hackathons etc.
  • Entrepreneurs
  • Student groups
  • Corporate recruiters

Why choose Converge?

Converge is made specifically for small and medium scale businesses, and helps you find team members for your startups, hackathons, and projects, with ease.
